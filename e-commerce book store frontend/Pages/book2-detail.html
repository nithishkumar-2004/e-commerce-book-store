The protagonist, Jonas, is a twelve-year-old boy chosen to become the "Receiver of Memory," a role that exposes him to memories of the world as it once was—full of both joy and suffering. Through his training with the current Receiver, known as "The Giver," Jonas begins to uncover the dark truths behind his community's strict rules and lack of emotion.

As Jonas learns more about the suppressed experiences of love, color, and freedom, he grapples with the moral implications of his society's sacrifices. His growing awareness leads him to a daring decision that could change his life—and the fate of the community—forever.
